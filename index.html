Dear group members,

Hello everyone! Welcome to our Minecraft Online Multiplayer Group.

I. Group Rules

Civilized Communication

Civilized language should be used in group communication. We should respect each other and it is prohibited to send any uncivilized content that violates public order and good customs.

Online Multiplayer Rules

When playing online multiplayer games, abide by the rules set by the organizers. Do not maliciously destroy others' achievements. When encountering problems, communicate and solve them calmly.

Group Chat Order

Don't flood the screen randomly. Try to focus the chat on game-related topics as much as possible.

II. Online Multiplayer Activities

Activity Organization

Online multiplayer activities will be organized irregularly in the group. Notice will be given in advance. We hope that everyone will actively participate.

Participation Methods

When there are online multiplayer activities, members who are interested in participating should reply with registration information on time and check the network and game version in advance.

III. Resource Sharing

Mods and Resources

Sharing high-quality resources is encouraged, but it must be legal and compliant. A simple description can be attached when sharing.

Original Works

Original works are welcomed to be shown in the group.

We hope that everyone will abide by the group rules and make our group a fun communication platform. Wish you all have a great time in Minecraft!
